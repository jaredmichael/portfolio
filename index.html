ABOUT ME

I'm a graduate of the Full Stack Engineering Program at Thinkful and am based in San Diego, CA. As a life-long learner, I love to explore new technologies and expand the abilities of my creativity. I am versed in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Redux, Node.Js, RESTful APIs and GitHub. I am passionate about front end design and I love seeing project ideas come to fruition on the screen.

Life is lived to learn and become your best self and this principle is something I strive to achieve in my personal and professional life everyday.
